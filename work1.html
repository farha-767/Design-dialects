Library house

The Library House is designed for a doctor couple and their two children, all avid readers. The entry to the site was challenging with the approach road steeply sloping down, which was managed by providing a large sliding gate deeply offset into the plot, giving space for a ramp to adjust the slope. The house blends to its surroundings with its earthen shades and simple form. The low height car porch with mid-level library above and a skewed formal living room define the shape of the house. An ever-flowering frangipani, extra wide steps and hues of natural slate on walls provide a welcoming entry to the house.

Location : Calicut, Kerala

Plot Area : 13.25 cents

Builtup Area : 3190 sqft
